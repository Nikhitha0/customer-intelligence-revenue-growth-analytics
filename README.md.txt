# Customer Intelligence & Revenue Growth Analytics

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior and revenue trends using SQL and Excel.

The objective was to identify:

* Top revenue generating countries
* Top revenue generating products
* High-value customers
* Customer segmentation groups
* Business opportunities for revenue growth

---

## Tools Used

* SQL (MySQL)
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Analysis
* Dashboard Creation

---

## Dataset

Online Retail Dataset containing:

* Transactions
* Customers
* Products
* Countries
* Revenue Information

---

## Business Questions Solved

### 1. Which countries generate the highest revenue?

Top 5 Countries:

* United Kingdom
* Netherlands
* EIRE
* Germany
* France

---

### 2. Which products generate the highest revenue?

Top Revenue Products:

* DOTCOM POSTAGE
* REGENCY CAKESTAND 3 TIER
* WHITE HANGING HEART T-LIGHT HOLDER
* PARTY BUNTING
* JUMBO BAG RED RETROSPOT

---

### 3. Who are the highest-value customers?

Top customers were identified based on total revenue generated.

---

### 4. Customer Segmentation

Customers were classified into:

* High Value Customers
* Medium Value Customers
* Low Value Customers

---

## Key Insights

* United Kingdom contributes the majority of revenue.
* Decorative and gift products dominate sales.
* Revenue is concentrated among a smaller group of customers.
* High-value customers represent the most important segment for retention.
